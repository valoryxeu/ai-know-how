# Research & Trends

Notable papers, breakthroughs, and emerging trends worth tracking.

## Topics

- **Papers** — summaries of important research papers
- **Trends** — emerging patterns and where AI is heading
- **Benchmarks** — how models are evaluated and compared
- **Scaling Laws** — what we know about model scaling
- **Safety & Alignment** — research on making AI safe and beneficial

## How I Capture Research

1. Read the paper / article / thread
2. Write a 3-5 sentence summary of the core insight
3. Note practical implications (how does this affect my work?)
4. Tag with relevant topics
5. Link to related concepts in this knowledge base
