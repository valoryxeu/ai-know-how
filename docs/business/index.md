# AI for Business

Strategic thinking about AI adoption, implementation, and value creation.

## Topics

- **Use Cases** — proven AI applications by industry and function
- **ROI Analysis** — measuring AI impact, cost-benefit frameworks
- **Implementation** — phased rollout, change management, training
- **Build vs Buy** — when to use APIs vs train custom models
- **Data Strategy** — data quality, governance, pipelines for AI
- **Ethics & Risk** — bias, fairness, transparency, regulatory compliance
- **Team Building** — AI roles, skills, hiring, upskilling

## Key Insight

> The biggest AI ROI comes from **automating high-volume, low-complexity decisions** — not from replacing human judgment on hard problems.
